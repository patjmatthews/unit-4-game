Crystal Game

How to play


  1. You will be given a random number to start"Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. Win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. When the game starts, the value changes.
